IMBULJUTA

Imbuljuta tal-Qastan (chestnut aromatic hot chocolate drink) is a traditional Maltese drink served after Midnight Mass on Christmas as well as on New Yearâ€™s Eve.

INGREDIENTS

200g chestnusts
Lemon and orange rind
1/2 tsp mixed spices
1/2 tsp ground cloves
Sugar (as much as you like)
1tbsp cocoa powder
100g dark cooking chocolate

Method

  1. Wash the chestnuts thoroughly. Place them in a large bowl and cover them with water. Leave to soak overnight.
  2. Next day remove any loose pieces of skin.
  3. Put the chestnuts in a deep pan and cover with water.
  4. Add all the above ingredient and cook over medium heat for around 1 hour.
  5. Chestnuts should be tender.
  6. Serve hot.
