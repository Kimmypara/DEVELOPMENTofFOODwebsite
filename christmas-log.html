CHRISTMAS LOG

2 hours
Serves 25
Easy
Download

Traditional Maltese Christmas Log is a delicious dessert for the holidays. It is a quick and easy, no bake recipe that you can prepare in under 30 minutes, the rest is waiting time.

INGREDIENTS

300g Morning Coffee biscuits
300g Nutella
50g chopped hazelnuts
50g tutti frutti
50g melted unsalted butter
100g peanut butter
50g cherries
250g white chocolate
1tsp cocoa powder to decorate

Method

  1. Crush the biscuits in a food processor and in another bowl add the chopped hazelnuts.
  2. In another bowl, melt the butter and peanut butter and add with the biscuits. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Add the Nutella, cherries and tutti frutti and mix with hands until a dough texture is formed.
  4. Form in a log shape.
  5. Cover in cling film and place into the refrigerator for 1 hour.
  6. Remove from the refrigerator and cover with melted chocolate. Decorate with cocoa powder and you can add some extra decorations as you wish or leave as is.
