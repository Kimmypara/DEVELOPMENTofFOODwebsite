CHRISTMAS LOG

2 hours
Serves 25
Easy

Traditional Maltese Christmas Log is a staple during the holidays. It is a quick and easy, no bake recipe that you can prep in under 15 minutes.

INGREDIENTS

500g Self Raising Flour
6 Eggs
375g margerine
2 bottles Lacto
300g brown sugar
4tbsp treacle
Juice of 1 lemon, 1 orange and 1 tangerine
Lemon and orange zest
1/2 tsp cinnammon powder
2 tbsp cocoa powder
200g dates
100g glaced cherries
500g black currants
300g sultana
200g hazelnuts

Method

  1. Place all the dried fruit,cherries, juices, zest, and Lacto in a large bowl and let soak for 1hour.
  2. Preheat oven to 150°C. Grease and line a 21 – 22 cm / 8 – 9" round cake pan with baking paper (parchment paper) (7 cm / 2.75" tall).
  3. In an electric mixer, mix the butter and brown sugar with a K Beater until creamy and fluffy.
  4. Slowly, add the treacle and the eggs 1 at a time.
  5. Sift the flour and cocoa and add slowly to mixture until combined. Do not overbeat.
  6. With a wooden spoon, add the dried fruit mixture and mix until combined.
  7. Bake in oven for around 2 1/2 hours or until a skewer inserted into middle comes out clean with no batter on it.
  8. Remove from oven and cool for 20 minutes before transferring to cooling rack. Cool completely before serving.
